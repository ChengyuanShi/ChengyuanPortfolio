About Me

CHENGYUAN SHI

My name is Chengyuan Shi and I am a Digital Media Arts graduate from San Jose State University. My work encompasses various fields including graphic design, UI design, interactive art, 3D modeling, and coding art.

My primary focus is on creating visually stunning pieces that engage the viewer through interactive design. Utilizing software like Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender, Cinema 4D, MaxMSP, Sketch, and Figma, I can transform my ideas into visually detailed artworks that leave a lasting impression on audiences.

Inspiration comes from my surroundings - life and the world around me. I enjoy observing and exploring diverse cultures and environments, incorporating these experiences into my artwork. As an artist, I continually evolve my style and experiment with new techniques and methods to create innovative and efficient pieces. I believe that digital media art is an infinitely creative field, and my hope is that my work can inspire others and bring new ideas to the forefront.
